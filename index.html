Experimental data exploration assignment 1

One of my favourite interactive data representatations is windy.com
It's a world map overlayed by wind direction and speed.
It's easy, intuative and very clear but with tons of options.
The arrows show direction and the color show speed.
Otherwise it works just like google maps. If you want more detailed information you
can just click on a point in the map.

The only bad example I can come up with would be kegg pathway graphs.
They are protein interaction pathways depicted by lines and protein names. All the nodes are links to other pages.
All the lines show what relates to eachothe.
It's so cluttered I always get a headache when I try too look up something. Maybe it could be improved by
adding some interactivity.
